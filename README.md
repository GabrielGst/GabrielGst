Former student of the Institute of Optics, I worked with python on signal and image processing projects (see IO-PTI) and design of optical systems with MATLAB and Zemax Optic Studio (see IO-COCO). Fond of IoT and web design, I worked on a personal project called Wind-Watch, which intends to be a sailing course web app that uses the weather data fetched by Coperniccus and the ECMWF (see winged-watch).

My natural interest in IT and software development—reinforced through a statistics track at the Institut d’Optique Graduate School (France) and a Data Engineer role at ESA—drives my ambition to contribute to Digital Transformation initiatives. I aim to work at the intersection of technology and organizational development to deliver impactful change.
